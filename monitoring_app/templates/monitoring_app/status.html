General status

Get INFO 



 
                  
To check the general status of your machine ,you need to click on the "Get status" bottom.
				  
The status will be presented us a color: Green or Orange or Red .
				  
Red means that :
				  
                  -There are a delay in packet send
				  
                  -Or used disck is more than 75%
				  
                  -Or there are no default route 
				  
Orange means that :
				  
                  -There are a voluminous file (more than 500Mo)
				  
                 -At least ,one of those services is OFF: DHCP or FreeSWITCH or DNS or ntp
				  
                 -There are a firewall rules 
				  
Green means that the machine is well. 
				  
To have an idéa about the real cause of the color, you must go to the "machine détail" module.
